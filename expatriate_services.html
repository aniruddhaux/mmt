Expatriate Services

Australian living abroad? You’re not alone.

Taxation Services

If you are an Australian living and working abroad or alternatively you are an individual planning to live in Australia, our highly specialized expat services can support the complex process of settling in a new country. Being on the ground in Australia we are able to bring a dynamic and relevant perspective to the advice and presentation of taxation and investment issues to our ex-pat clients and international audiences.

We can help you by providing expats with:

  • Taxation and Accounting Advice
  • Auditing
  • Business Succession Strategies
  • Financial planning and Investment advice
  • Superannuation advice
  • Life insurance and asset protection
  • Retirement planning
